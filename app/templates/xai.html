What are SHAP values?

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) is a game-theoretic approach to explain the outputs of any machine learning model. It connects optimal allocation of credit with local explanations using classical Shapley values.

Each feature value acts as a "player" in a game where the prediction is the payout. SHAP values distribute the payout among the features fairly, according to their marginal contributions.

How to read local waterfall charts:

  • Green values push the prediction upward (indicating high potential gaps, good catchments, or size factors).
  • Red values drag the prediction downward (indicating supply caps, low growth, or high local competition).
  • The chart starts at the Base Value (average log prediction) and sums the steps to reach the final Prediction Log.
